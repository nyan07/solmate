# Competition & Market

## Direct Competitors

### SunSeekr — London/Europe

- URL: https://sunseekr.com
- Launched 2025 in London, available as browser + iOS app
- Shows sun/shade on map, helps find sunny cafés/pubs/restaurants
- Only registered businesses can add additional info about their location
- Basic map shows locations with shade/sun overlay

### Jveuxdusoleil — Paris

- URL: https://jveuxdusoleil.fr
- French equivalent, Paris-focused

## Adjacent Tools (Different Use Cases)

These apps combine maps + sun data but are not B2C gastronomy focused:

- **Photographers** — predicting sun/shadow for photoshoots
- **Garden designers** — understanding sunny/shaded garden zones

UI on these tools is very technical, not appealing for everyday consumer use.

## Arkie's Differentiation

- Consumer-friendly UI
- Combines sun data with curated gastronomy (Google Places)
- 3D building shadow modeling via Cesium (not just flat sun overlays)
- Filters: open/closed, outdoor seating, sunny/shaded
- Mobile-first (PWA, iOS Safari optimised)
