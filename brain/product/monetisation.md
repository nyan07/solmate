# Monetisation

## Ideas (not yet implemented)

- **Per-app purchase** — one-time payment to unlock
- **Business promotion** — cafés/bars pay to appear more prominently in search/list results
- **Verified places** — businesses pay to claim and manage their profile

## Future Considerations

- B2B angle: allow businesses to modify their own profile data
- Notification upsell: alert users when a favourite location becomes sunny
